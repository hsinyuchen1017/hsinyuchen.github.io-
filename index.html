Research

I'm interested in game theory, machine learning, deep learning, speech signal processing, and time series analysis.

Heterogeneous Star Celebrity Games
Yen-Yu Chang, Chin-Chia Hsu, Ho-Lin Chen,
Preprint, 2018

In this paper, we study the problem of heterogeneous star celebrity games and we prove the upper bound of PoA.
ANS: Adaptive Network Scaling for Deep Rectifier Reinforcement Learning Models
Yueh-Hua Wu, Fan-Yun Sun, Yen-Yu Chang, Shou-De Lin,
Will be submitted to the 2019 European Conference on Machine Learning and Principles and Practice of Knowledge Discovery in Databases (ECML PKDD 2019), 2019
arXiv / bibtex

We also propose an Adaptive Network Scaling framework to find a suitable scale of the rewards during learn- ing for better performance. We conducted empirical studies to justify the solution.
A Memory-Network Based Solution for Multivariate Time-Series Forecasting
Yen-Yu Chang, Fan-Yun Sun, Yueh-Hua Wu, Shou-De Lin,
Will be submitted to the 2019 International Joint Conference on Artificial Intelligence (IJCAI-19), 2019
arXiv / bibtex

Inspired by Memory Network proposed for solving the question-answering task, we propose a deep learning based model named Memory Timeseries network (MTNet) for time series forecasting.
Noise-to-Noise Speech Enhancement: Speech Denoising without Clean Speech
Yen-Yu Chang, Jing-Cheng Chang, Hung-Yi Lee,
Submitted to 2019 IEEE International Conference on Acoustic, Speech, and Signal Processing (ICASSP 2019), 2019

With statistical arguments, we show that theoretically even without using the uncorrupted speech data, it is possible to transform the corrupted speech into its clean counterpart
Designing Non-greedy Reinforcement Learning Agents with Diminishing Reward Shaping
Fan-Yun Sun, Yen-Yu Chang, Yueh-Hua Wu, Shou-De Lin,
Published in 2018 AAAI/ACM Conference on Artificial Intelligence, Ethics, and Society (AIES-18), 2018
bibtex

We propose the idea of diminishing reward to train non-greedy agents in multi-agent reinforcement learning scenarios with nearly no extra cost
